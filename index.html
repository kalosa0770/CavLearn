Computer Science

  • Introduction to Algorithms

    Authors: Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein

    A comprehensive book that covers a wide range of algorithms in depth, ideal for both beginners and advanced students.

  • Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach

    Authors: Stuart Russell, Peter Norvig

    This book is a leading textbook in the field of artificial intelligence, covering both theory and practice in AI.

  • Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship

    Author: Robert C. Martin

    This book provides best practices for writing clean, maintainable, and efficient code, essential for software developers.

Economics

  • Principles of Economics

    Author: N. Gregory Mankiw

    A fundamental textbook that introduces the principles of microeconomics and macroeconomics, suitable for beginners.

  • The Wealth of Nations

    Author: Adam Smith

    This classic book lays the foundation of modern economic theory and discusses the role of self-interest in economic life.

  • Freakonomics: A Rogue Economist Explores the Hidden Side of Everything

    Authors: Steven D. Levitt, Stephen J. Dubner

    An unconventional book that applies economic theory to diverse, real-world scenarios, making economics accessible and fun.

Business Administration

  • Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap... and Others Don't

    Author: Jim Collins

    This book explores the key factors that allow some companies to achieve long-term success and others to falter.

  • The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses

    Author: Eric Ries

    A must-read for entrepreneurs and business students, this book introduces lean principles to startup development.

  • Harvard Business Review on Managing Yourself

    Authors: Various

    A collection of articles from Harvard Business Review, providing insights into personal and professional development.

    Authors: Kenneth Lysons, Brian Farrington

  • Supply Chain Management: Strategy, Planning, and Operation

    Author: Sunil Chopra, Peter Meindl
